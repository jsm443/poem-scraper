<div id="poem3711979" class="poem poem-left preview" data-align="left" data-url="http://hellopoetry.com/poem/3711979/all-perfect-days-end/" data-text="All Perfect Days End by Colm" seepoem="/act/poem/see/3711979/" readpoem="/act/poem/read/3711979/">

      </div>
      <div class="preview-stats s subtle"></div>
      <div class="preview-actions"></div>
    </div>
  
  <div class="poem-view inner">
    <div class="poem-header poem-part">
      
      
      
        <a href="/colmistoirm/" class="nocolor poem-poet-name">Colm</a>
      
      
        
          <span class="poem-added s" title="Poem added Feb 11, 2020">

          </span>
        
      
    </div>

    <div class="poem-part poem-title bottomss">
      <a href="/poem/3711979/all-perfect-days-end/" class="nocolor">All Perfect Days End</a>
    </div>

    <div class="poem-part continue-reading poem-body wordwrap">
      As<br>a sign<br>Sometimes<br>it all goes right <br>The stars don't align <br>but I feel them nonetheless <br>And for a moment life's duress is but a memory <br>To me, as I live and burn in this present candlelight <br>Though sometimes the evening is even darker than the night<br>And I crash into the atmosphere of a more realistic Me <br>Like a lonely poet who never shares his paint<br>Like a bygone song no instrument will play<br>And in burning I retreat <br>to the halls of sleep<br>Sometimes<br>I am far<br>away<br><br>(Even though it seemed to be the perfect day)<br>
